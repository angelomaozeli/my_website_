· Initiated industry research, reviewed announcements during issuance process, collected stock market information and authored 3 stock market analysis reports during issuance process for decision-making of senior management

· Facilitated due diligence, documentation and continuous supervision fieldwork of Private Placement of Hainan Haide plc, organised working paper catalogue and created the documentation archive for the whole project (1000+ pages)

· Established database of shareholder structure and financial summary of 40+ domestic asset management companies, coordinated background check of 11 potential clients in prepare for potential projects with approaching IPO clients

· Created financial spreadsheet models, collected data and analysed relevant information using accounting, financial and legal knowledge, Bloomberg terminal and Wind Database and other finance resources
